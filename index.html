Our Story

Burger opened in 1989 with a simple goal: serve the best handcrafted burgers using fresh, local ingredients, we've grown from a small family restaurant to the city's favorite burger spot with five locations. We grind our beef fresh daily, bake our buns in-house, and source locally whenever possible. Named "Best Burger in the City" three years straight, we're committed to quality food and genuine hospitality. From our family to yours, welcome to Burger.
